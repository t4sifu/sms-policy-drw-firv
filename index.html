SMS Market News Alerts Opt-In

This page describes how users can opt in to receive SMS market news alerts.

How to Subscribe

To subscribe, text START to +1 (888) 887-3928.

After texting START, users will receive a confirmation message asking them to confirm enrollment.

Service Description

Subscribers receive recurring automated SMS alerts containing real-time financial market news, macroeconomic updates, geopolitical headlines, commodity market developments, central bank commentary, earnings headlines, and other time-sensitive market-moving events.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on market activity and user alert settings.

Disclosures

Message and data rates may apply. Consent is not a condition of purchase.

Help and Opt-Out

Reply HELP for help.

Reply STOP to unsubscribe at any time.

Example Messages

  • BREAKING: OPEC signals possible production adjustment.
  • RUBIO: WE CONTINUE TO WORK ON IRAN DIPLOMACY.
  • FED OFFICIAL COMMENTS MOVE TREASURY FUTURES.
